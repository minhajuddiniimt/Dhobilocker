How it works?

In 4 Easy Steps



Step 1: Drop-off
  • - Customers will place clothes in locker & locks it with random 4 Digit PIN.
  • - Places Order via dhobilocker mobile app by choosing required laundry services.


Step 2 : Collection
  • - Dhobi will collect all orders at once using master PIN.


Step 3 : Delivery
  • - Delivers it back by setting up a new PIN.


Step 4 : Pick-up
  • - Customer will receive delivery notification.
  • - After Payment receives locker number and PIN for pick-up
